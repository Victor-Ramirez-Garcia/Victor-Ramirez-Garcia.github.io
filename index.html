About Me

Greetings, my name is Victor U. Ramirez-Garcia, I am a nineteen-year-old student currently pursuing my bachelors degree in Computer Science at Portland State University with the intent on getting a full-time job in the CS department after graduating. I have spent a little over 4 years designing and programming projects with HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript. From creating interactive and educational projects to getting a scholarship to become a Full-Stack developer, I realized that CS is going to become my passion. My older brother was passionate about working and creating with wood and metal throughout his high school years. I watched and tried unsuccessfully to imitate him in his classes but I discovered my passion lies in creating projects online.

Moving my passion for CS aside, I enjoy exercising from time to time in order to cool off steam by doing some resistance and strength training. I always try to find ways to create new projects. I usually go for interactive projects because I find that to be more difficult to do. Later this year I am revisiting my project “Imaze (Remastered)” and redo the whole project again with the current knowledge I have. I have spent some time learning Object-Oriented Programming in Javascript and I think redoing my maze project is a great way to build up my OOP skills.
